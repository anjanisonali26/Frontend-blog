My Favorite Place

This lake is one of the masterpieces of natural beauty that is bestowed by the Almighty on the land of East Java. Before heading to the peak of Semeru, it is at this lake location that climbers will usually prepare tents to rest or spend the night. While gathering energy, they can enjoy the beauty of the lake or explore the beautiful natural scenery around with a camera. For those of you who have never been to Ranu Kumbolo and are interested in setting foot there, before leaving, please refer to some important information regarding Kumbolo Lake in the following article. This lake is located in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park (TNBTS).....

Located at an altitude of appoximately 2,400 meters above sea level, the air temperature in this area is very cool and comfortable to relax. If you have time to come to the location of this lake during the holiday season, you will find many climbers or tourists who deliberately come just to camp and spend their holiday time in this place. Ranu Kumbolo does offer a beauty that is second to none. In addition to the exotic form of the lake that spoils the eye, in the afternoon we can also watch the sunset moment that appears between the peaks of two mountains from the lake location. In addition, for visitors who like photography, this place is a paradise to channel that hobby.
